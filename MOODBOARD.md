# Moodboard – HighFire Spellcraft Arena

Bildgenerator-Prompts zur visuellen Richtungsfindung für Atmosphäre, Gameplay-Momente und Crafting-UX.
Die Prompts sind bewusst auf Englisch gehalten, da Bildgeneratoren (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion) damit besser arbeiten.

---

## 1. „Arcane Foundry" – Arena-Atmosphäre

**Stimmung**: Ritualhaft, industriell-fantastisch, kraftvoll und bewusst. Das Zentrum der Arena ist immer der visuelle Fokuspunkt.

> A dramatic top-down cinematic view of a futuristic mage arena, dark obsidian floor covered in glowing neon rune cracks in electric blue and violet, floating spell-crafting glyphs orbiting the arena center, two silhouetted warrior-mage figures holding glowing staff-blade hybrids, particle streams trailing from their hands forming combat sigils, volumetric purple fog rising from the floor edges, metallic arena walls with embedded arcane circuitry, rim lighting in warm amber contrasting with cool blue magic, photorealistic concept art, ultra-detailed, 8K, cinematic composition, game concept art style

> Action shot inside a collapsing arena, stone floor tiles mid-explosion with glowing orange fissures underneath, debris and rocks frozen in mid-air, two fast-moving player characters blurred by speed, glowing red and cyan target-lock HUD rings around opponents, electric arcs jumping between crumbling pillars, dramatic overhead spotlight cutting through volumetric dust clouds, neon impact craters where spells hit the ground, dark background with distant glowing city ruins, hyper-detailed game screenshot aesthetic, dynamic action composition, matte painting style, UE5 quality

> Close-up stylized illustration of two hands gripping a game controller, glowing motion-trail sigils emerging from thumbstick gestures forming a magical spell symbol in the air above, holographic HUD panels floating nearby showing spell ingredient slots and weapon upgrade nodes, metallic controller surface reflecting spell glow in purple and orange gradients, background softly blurred showing arena action, combo input sequence visualized as glowing rune chain, cyberpunk meets dark fantasy aesthetic, high contrast lighting, concept art for a AAA action RPG, 4K resolution, detailed digital painting

## 4. „Warrior Silhouette" – Charakter-Design

**Stimmung**: Kraftvolle, klar lesbare Spieler-Silhouetten. Hybrid aus Krieger und Magier. Kein übertriebenes Detail – Farbe und Glühen definieren Identität.

> Side-view character silhouette design for a fast-paced arena mage-warrior, body rendered as a clean dark silhouette with glowing cyan rim light outlining the entire figure, one hand holding a staff-sword hybrid weapon glowing in electric blue, the other hand trailing magical particle streams, minimalist geometric armor shapes built from simple polygons, no texture detail only color and light, the character stance is dynamic and aggressive mid-dodge, background pure black, 4 color variants shown side by side (cyan, magenta, green, gold) each with matching weapon glow, game character concept art, flat design meets neon glow aesthetic, 4K

**Stimmung**: Schwebende holographische Elemente, minimalistisch, hoher Kontrast. Kein unnötiger Rahmen oder Dekoration – reine Information in Licht.

## 6. „Main Menu Screen" – Menü-Atmosphäre
